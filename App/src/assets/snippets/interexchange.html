Are you ready to take your career to the next level? InterExchange Intern USA is a cultural exchange program that enables university students and recent graduates from around the world to elevate their careers with professional-level internships in the United States for up to 12 months. InterExchange, a New York City-based nonprofit organization, is designated by the U.S. Department of State to sponsor a variety of J-1 Visa Exchange Visitor programs and is the exclusive partner of the Erasmus Student Network (ESN) for J-1 Visa sponsorship of U.S. internships.

If you have already found a U.S. internship in your field of study, InterExchange can help you take advantage of global opportunities as you gain practical experience in your field and learn American methodologies and technology. You'll enjoy speedy application processing, low program fees, ongoing support throughout your program, and access to our alumni network.
